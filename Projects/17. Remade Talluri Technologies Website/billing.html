Medical Billing Process

Talluri follows strategies by providing end to end billing solutions including patient registration, verifying patient eligibility, prior authorization, charge entry, payment posting, accounts receivables & patient follow ups.

Patient Demographic contains all the basic demographic information about an individual or patient. We have well trained professionals to capture and verifies the entire patient demographic and insurance details; before updating the information in PMS & submits the clean claims to the insurance company’s.

Charge entry is one of the crucial areas in medical billing; where you can create your claims. To get process your insurance claims, need to provide the patient details like face sheet, physician details, insurance coverage and billing information, which is taken care by Talluri.

Talluri provides an electronic claims transmission to all Federal and Non Federal payers through the clearing house by submitting the primary & secondary insurance claims.

The benefits of the electronic claims transmission:
  • Faster claims revenue cycle
  • Submitting claim instantly to the payer
  • Saves time and paper printing
  • Increase practice efficiency

Payment posting in medical billing is one of the key processes that get the utmost attention from our operations management. We post the payments & denials in to the patient account which receive in all forms, i.e. EOB’s, EFT’s received from carriers, checks, credit card and cash payments received from patients. Posting team forwards the denial claims information to AR team; so AR team works on denials the day we receive the EOB which minimizes the AR days

Though we are processing secondary claims electronically, it is require to print paper claims also; as there are some smaller insurance carriers are not able to accept electronic claims so, we print & mail out Secondary paper claims to reimburse the payment which left by primary insurance. We involve into printing the CMS & UB-04 paper claims.
